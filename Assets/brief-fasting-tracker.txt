brief:
fasting tracker

core concept:
fasting timer, user selects fast type:
    -16/8, 18/6, 20/4.
-lets them know when fast ends/ when to eat
    -display countdown/ time fast ends
-track the users progress/ previous fasts
    -user tells app when they started fasting
        -by pressing button 
            -or-
        -manually entering time

    -user tells the app when they ended the fast
        -by pressing button to end
            -or-
        -manually enter time ended
-bar graph daily progress  for the week, hours fasted, hours eating.
-user posts to twitter from app to share progress to twitter.

added functions:
-recipe finder
-fasting advice
-get tweets from fitness people.
